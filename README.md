### about me <br>

I'm a Machine Learning Lead and Machine Learning research scientist. In my job I work on deploying AI to marketing teams, and my research interests centre on covering NLP and neurocomputation for logical reasoning tasks. Previously, I was an ML researcher at Eigen Technologies (part of Sirion), a Research Fellow in the O'Keefe Lab (Sainsbury Wellcome Centre). I completed my PhD in the Bizley Lab (UCL Ear Institute), where I TA-ed for Web Development and Software Engineering within the UCL CS Department. I'm most interested in generalisation in all of its forms, including whether if (argubaly) LLMs can generalise or pick up human-like mechanisms of 'thinking'. This profile contains my public work and old work when I was in academia.

In my PhD/post-doc, I studied: <br>
1. applying machine learning techniques to solve neuroscience problems and improve artificial neural networks (esp. w.r.t. spatial navigation and sensory perception)
2. developing software for neuroscience research, particularly for spike sorting and classification 🧠 📊

Feel free to reach out via [email](mailto:carla.griffiths.16@ucl.ac.uk) if you have any questions about my work or check out my personal site in my profile!
